  • How to provide student centric online education

Ever wondered what separates a highly successful online course from the rest? Or about the practices you might need to incorporate to turn your own course into one of the successful ones?

Well according to numerous experienced instructors, creating a student-centric learning experience is one way to go about it.

To take a student-centred approach,ask yourself the following questions

  1. (Graphic labelled student-student interaction) How will my students interact with each other and build a student community?
  2. (Graphic labelled student-teacher interaction) How will my students approach me with doubts and/or suggestions they might have?
  3. (Graphic labelled student-content interaction) How will my students interact with the course material besides simply reading it?

Student-centered learning creates a highly engaging and collaborative environment for your students. It has a huge role in keeping students motivated to keep learning!

  • Benefits of Collaboration Software

Online learning has many advantages but also a few accompanying disadvantages such as inhibition of relationship formation and self-development.

Imagine a class of students, where each student is being taught the same course material but separately, at their own personal desks. After the lesson is taught, the teacher gives them a quiz to check their comprehension and then everyone goes about their day. The students don’t have another incentive to interact with the course material, the teacher has no way of understanding what adjustments need to be made to customise the material in a way that’s favourable to everybody.

Such a learning environment can prove to be very ineffective, and to overcome this it is wise to take help of collaboration software.

Collaboration software helps in adding a human touch in digital learning programs by providing features that enable productive communication and collaboration between all the members of a community. While it enables students to learn with and from-one another, it also proves useful to teachers as it helps them to get vital feedback, adapt and improve their courses. To learn about the advantages that collaboration software present to online course creators, read EZColab’s blog “Benefits of Using Collaboration Software to Engage Your Students” (LINK TO BLOG)
