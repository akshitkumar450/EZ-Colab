10 Best Online Course Niches

By lorem ipsum
Nov 10,2021

Are you in search of a profitable course idea? This article is here to provide you with some measure of assistance.

Finding a topic idea that you feel excited about teaching as well as one which is profitable enough to result in substantial capital gains are important aspects to consider at the beginning of your online course creation journey. A simple search on Google Trends shows that the demand of online courses in various fields of study is growing steadily. This greatly encourages online instructors to build and launch an online course in the niche they are most competent in and feel passionate about. Creating an online course also helps instructors in expanding their professional network beyond geographical borders.

Due to the Coronavirus pandemic in early 2020, there was a sharp increase in the number of people searching for online courses to help them gain new skills and/or utilize their time productively. There are numerous benefits that motivate people to sign up for an online education. Some of the most commonly stated benefits of pursuing an online course are:

  1. Online courses and course certificates help in skilling-up and enhancing your technical prowess
  2. You get acquainted with the latest trends in your domain
  3. They can help in getting a promotion at your job
  4. Acquiring a hands-on training experience

If you are in the process of figuring out what subject you want to start creating a course on, you are in the right place. We have curated a list of the 10 best online course ideas for you to consider. Read on further to gain an idea of the various niches of online courses that are in demand and select the topic that feels right for you!

1. Business Courses

Business and entrepreneur courses are one of the most sought after online courses and for a good reason too! Business courses help an individual in honing their communication, management and entrepreneur skills and are a great way to give one's resume a boost. Google Trends shows that every year a vast number of people sign up for various kinds of business courses in order to add additional value in their academic journey or gain a leg up in their career. The rise in the demand of freelancers in the market and the increasing number of start-ups are also established causes of the high value demand of business courses

If you are looking to create a business course you can consider one of the following most searched for topics:-

  1. Finance and Accounting
  2. Business Law
  3. Sales and Marketing
  4. Project Management
  5. Operations
  6. Human Resources
  7. Fundamental Business Skills
  8. Fundamental Business Skills
  9. Circular economics
  10. eCommerce
2. Computer Programming and Development based Courses

Given the vast amount of automation that is now part of every person's daily life, computer programming has become a very valuable skill to have. Technology is constantly avoiding and so are the various computer languages that bring those technologies to life. Different programming languages are used for different use cases such as cybersecurity, ethical hacking, web development and design etc. If you are someone with a software engineering background you probably know more than one programming language and multiple software applications however, if you are thinking of sharing your knowledge with the world in form of an online course we would suggest going with the topic that you are most passionate about. This will greatly help you drive the course creation process into completion.

Some of the most popular topics that you can build your course on in the computer science niche are listed as follows:-

  1. Data Science
  2. Machine Learning and Artificial Intelligence
  3. Cybersecurity
  4. Big Data
  5. Business Intelligence Analysis
  6. Cloud Computing
  7. Software Development
  8. Full-stack Development
  9. DevOps
3. Language Learning

Ever since the novel coronavirus pandemic resulted in lockdowns being imposed in various countries across the globe, there has been a surge in the number of people looking for language courses online. The primary reason for this increase in number was the desire to make productive use of the time at home and learn a new skill. In this case, a new language.

Learning a new language presents a number of benefits to the student. It has the potential to boost their career prospects, school or college education, and facilitate an easier time while studying abroad in an age of ever-growing globalization. It might also come in handy while one is travelling and exploring the culture of the place they are in!

The top five global languages are English, Mandarin, Hindi, Spanish and French. Not surprisingly a professional English course is one of the most sought after language courses. Here is a compiled list of the best language courses to create:-

  1. English
  2. French
  3. Spanish
  4. German
  5. Japanese
  6. Mandarin
  7. Italian
  8. Russian
4. Creative Arts

Online creative arts classes are continually gaining momentum and therefore find themselves in the category of highly profitable online classes. Boosting one's creativity finds importance in various career fields from artists, writers to engineers and scientists. By building an engaging course centered around creative arts and crafts you can directly impact hundreds of individuals from different walks of life.

There are various sub-topics in this niche and you should pick a category which you are most passionate about while keeping in mind the market demand of your chosen category. Here is a comprehensive list of the various types of courses you can create:

  1. Art
    1. Drawing ( Animation, Illustrations, Caricatures etc.)
    2. Sketching and Painting (Pencil colours, water or oil-based paints etc.)
  2. Music
    1. Online lessons on musical instruments (Guitar, Piano, Cello etc.)
    2. Online vocal lessons (Classical, Folk, etc.)
  3. Photography
    1. Digital Photography
    2. Portrait Photography
    3. Landscape Photography
5. Fitness Courses

The global pandemic caused a huge disruption in the profit percentages of the fitness industry. Gyms and physical fitness centers across the world were left ailing and many such businesses had to shut down permanently. People looking to keep physically fit even while they were shut inside their homes opted for at-home fitness which came in the form of online fitness courses, app subscriptions etc.

According to a report by the World Economic Forum, India had the highest increase of fitness app downloads at 156% as well as the largest increase in daily active users with 84% more people using fitness based apps each day [1]. New research data is claiming that this presents a huge opportunity for fitness trainers to capitalise on. If you are a fitness expert and are looking to take your business online, here are a few online course ideas for you to start working on:

  1. Strength and Muscle building home workouts
  2. Martial Arts Class
  3. Cardiovascular Workouts
  4. Dance and Zumba Class
  5. Yoga and Pilates Class
6. Personal Development

Personal development is a way for individuals to maximize their potential and reach their target goals in life through introspection and working towards building new skills in life. It has been observed that self-help books and books about self-improvement perform consistently well in the market. With self-improvement courses, people can learn how to enhance their quality of life, develop their capabilities and realize their dreams and aspirations.

A statistics report by Market Research reports that by 2022 the self improvement industry state will have an estimated growth of $13.2 billion with 5.6% average yearly gains [2]. Now, more than ever, people are focused on improving the quality of their lives by working on self-improvement. Hence, this is a lucrative industry to enter into through the medium of an online academy or a series of individual courses.

If you think you are up for the challenge of being a life-coach and that you can help guide others to become a better version of themselves, check out the following online course ideas in this domain:

  1. Time Management Course
  2. Conflict Navigation Course
  3. Public Speaking Improvement Class
  4. Cooking Skills and Nutrition Class
  5. Professional Networking Class
7. Health

Nowadays, an increasing number of people living in urban areas are becoming conscious of their health. Consequently, courses that can help people attain an understanding of the best practices to lead a healthy lifestyle are growing in popularity. The main goal of introducing potential customers to new and better health practices is to help them transform their lives. Health based courses are therefore expected to be backed by thorough scientific research and to be taught in such a way that people find them easy to follow.

Here is a list of options that you can choose to create a course about:

  1. Nutrition and Food Habits
  2. Special Diets (Vegan, Keto, Paleo etc)
  3. Meal Preparation to suit the urban lifestyle
  4. Whole Food and Raw Food Nutrition
  5. Diet Supplements
8. Content Creation and Writing

Content Writing is flowering into a lucrative career option with content development being an essential need for digital businesses. High-quality, engaging and informative content can directly impact the quantity and quality of customer traffic for your business. Content writers are expected to pump out consistently good content and apply several search engine optimisation techniques to ensure that their content ranks closer to the top. If you are someone with an impressive knowledge of creative writing, online marketing and know how to help online businesses perform better, you can divulge your knowledge to other keen learners who might be hoping to venture into the world of content writing and creation.

Examples of some popular courses falling in this niche are listed below:

  1. Creative Writing
  2. Blogging
  3. Search Engine Optimisation (SEO) and Search Engine Marketing (SEM)
  4. Writing an e-book
  5. Freelance Writing
9. Office Productivity

Office productivity tools are software applications that facilitate the viewing, creating, altering and inserting general office documents such as official letters, spreadsheets, presentations, databases, memos etc. Productivity tools are widely employed in offices and therefore it is imperative for employees to be well versed in knowing how to use them to increase their efficiency at work. For instance, using a simple database management application like MS Excel can make the tasks of storing data and applying different mathematical operations on it so much more efficient than using a calculator and doing it manually. You can build a big online community of people desiring to learn such office productivity tools.

Here is a list of some topics that you can build a course in this domain:

  1. MS Word
  2. MS Excel
  3. MS PowerPoint
  4. Data Visualisation
  5. Data Modelling
  6. Project Planning
10. Teaching and Academics

If you are a teacher with a rich experience in the education sector who does not want to be bound by geographical boundaries to share their expertise on a particular subject, you are a perfect candidate for this online course idea. There is a high demand for well-curated courses on subjects covered in schools, and colleges. Courses helping in preparation of competitive exams are also very popular. These include preparatory courses for exams such as GRE, GMAT, IELTS, TOEFL etc. English language testing exams such as IELTS and TOEFL have 3.5 million and 2.3 million test takers per year. Over half a million individuals across 160 countries take the GRE General Test every year while more than 300,000 people opt for GMAT.

There is no limit to the number of subjects that can be taught via online courses but here we list a set of most popular and in-demand subjects of them all:

  1. English grammar
  2. Mathematics and Statistics
  3. Media Training
  4. Science
  5. Philosophy
  6. Online course creation
  7. Entrance Exams Preparatory Courses
  8. Social science research

It is important that the subject you select is one that you are competent in and possess good knowledge about. It greatly adds to your credit if you are a certified professional in the chosen subject area and have a few years worth of experience under your belt.
