The Best Email Marketing Tools For Your Online Course

By lorem ipsum
Nov 10,2021

Email marketing is an unparalleled digital marketing strategy. For teachers and instructors, it is about more than just promoting their courses by sending unlimited emails—it is a way to easily connect with students and simplify their online teaching journey. Selecting the right email marketing tools is a crucial step in advertising your online course. Email marketing tools enable a user to create, implement and monitor their email campaign. Some email marketing applications are equipped with email content builders, scheduling options, and others have advanced features including marketing automation tools that can save the user from repetitive tasks. With these tools, the users can also get a better insight into important performance metrics that they can utilize to update their marketing strategy for the best results. Before jumping into the best tools available for email marketing of your online course let us comprehend what email marketing is and why it is considered an essential distribution medium.

What is Email Marketing?

Email marketing simply refers to the use of emails to convert leads into sales by sending personalized business messages to the target audience. Email marketing is a decades-old marketing channel that is considered to be one of the most potent marketing channels. In spite of being an old-school marketing medium, it is considered anything but that. Even in 2021, it proves to be one of the most reliable and popular online channels to promote your product or service. A research report on Statista states that in 2020, approximately 306 billion emails were sent and received every day worldwide. This figure is projected to increase to over 376 billion daily emails in 2025 [1]. Statistics state that as many as 87% of marketers use promotional emails for content dissemination [2]. With this email marketing statistic in mind, it is unwise to ignore the potential of a powerful email marketing campaign.

Consider the case of Harry’s [3], a business that sells personal grooming products and shaving tools through both digital and retail stores. Their subscription model offers monthly or weekly delivery of products chosen based on the customer’s unique profile and skincare needs.

Since its inception in 2013, the brand has gone to great lengths to ensure that its direct-to-consumer (DTC) product is a success. As a result, Harry’s has built a brand worth $1.4B.

What They Did: According to a case study of their marketing, among their 5-prong marketing approach, their most interesting strategy was their very first email marketing campaign. All 12 of their employees sent emails to everyone they knew in their very own personal and professional networks. In only a week, they grew their customer list to 100,000 subscribers using this incredibly simple direct marketing tactic. They then set up a dedicated landing page and followed it up with giving away offers and discounts!

There are several benefits associated with email marketing. Some of the most important ones are:

  1. It is a cost-effective method as compared to other digital marketing tools.
  2. It has a high Return on Investment (ROI) [4]
  3. You own your media and contact lists.
  4. Assistance in creating targeted messages for your audience
  5. Helps in the collection of customer feedback.
Marketing your Online Course using Email Campaigns

In order to promote and implement your online course or online course software in an effective manner, you need strong email marketing tools that can help reach your cold contacts and current students with customized messages.

In this post, we have compiled a list of the best email marketing tools that are available to you and that can hopefully assist you in executing the perfect email marketing strategy for your course!

1. EmailOctopus

EmailOctopus is an email marketing software tool that provides a highly cost-effective way of sending emails to your target audience while also providing an excellent platform to develop your marketing campaign. It is built on the back of Amazon's email service provider called Simple Email Service (SES), hence excellent deliverability of emails is guaranteed.

Pricing:
Free Plan Paid Plan
$0/month (Upto 2500 subscribers and 10,000 emails per month) Starting from $20/ month

Some of the outstanding features that EmailOctopus offers are listed below:

  1. Easy Creation of Sign-up Forms:

    2. You can create customized subscription forms asking a visitor to enter their email address to sign up for your services. EmailOctopus also allows the use of their official plugin to add a subscription form to your WordPress site.

  2. Multiple Integration Features:

    3. You can also connect various applications in your arsenal to this platform such as OptinMonster for lead generation, ClickFunnels for creating more sales funnels among others.

  3. Hassle-free set up of Landing Pages:

    4. EmailOctopus provides an in-built feature to set up beautiful landing pages on its platform. It also permits the user to design more advanced pages via their integrations with other third-party service providers.

A major disadvantage that is associated with EmailOctopus is listed below:

  1. Lack of advanced automation functions:

    2. There is an unavailability of advanced automated functions such as a function to create customer segments based on subscriber data.

2. Mailchimp

Mailchimp is an excellent option for personal and small to medium business users. Most of the tools and templates are available for free that provide a satisfactory result. These include visual email templates with video content which is known to perform better with the target customers. They also offer flexible payment options, which prove to be cheaper for low-volume clients, whilst also providing you with some extra features such as automation, integration options and setting up drip emails. They have free plans as well as paid plans that start from $9.99/month.

Pricing:
Free Plan Paid Plan
$0/month (Upto 2000 subscribers) Starting from $10.49/month

Here is a list of some of the features of MailChimp:

  1. Offers Basic Analytics:

    2. It offers data analytics that are helpful for improving and updating inbound marketing. This includes Google Analytics integration that allows for conversion tracking, geo-tracking and social media tracking.

  2. Most Generous Free Plan:

    3. Their free plan is equipped with most of the tools that are required and the user is allowed 10,000 emails to 2000 of their subscribers, which is a pretty good deal.

  3. Automated Drip Campaigns

    4. MailChimp puts emphasis on opt-in campaigns and one of the features that they offer to accomplish this is their automated drip campaigns. By sending drip emails you can boost sales by engaging casual visitors into loyal customers or subscribers.

The major disadvantage associated with using MailChimp:

  1. High Cost of Subscription:

    2. Once users start needing a higher number of emails or have an increasing number of subscribers the pricing for the paid plans increases sharply. Its most basic paid plan starts from $10.49/month and the most premium plan starts from $313.22/month

3. ActiveCampaign

ActiveCampaign is a popular email marketing software tool and for a good reason too. It caters to varied forms of customers from small, medium to enterprises. ActiveCampaign is now associated with 145,000+ businesses in 170 countries. It lays emphasis on deepening connections with one's customers at every contact point and hence does not hold "vanity metrics" or sending bulk emails in high regard. The email marketing automation features offered on this platform are impressive and their premium plan has proven itself to be worth the fee according to several businesses.

Pricing:
Free Plan Paid Plan
Unavailable Starting from $9/month

If you are interested in checking out ActiveCampaign's services have a look at some of its features:

  1. Powerful and easy to use Automation features:

    2. One of ActiveCampaign's most important selling points is automation. You have access to auto-responders, which can be set on various conditions, you can automate list management and contact management. It also allows you to automate follow-up campaigns, edit multiple conditions, come up with personalized emails and the corresponding email subject etc.

  2. Facilitation of Sales Funnel Creation:

    3. The Conversations feature of this platform is noteworthy. You can send targeted messages directly to your page or website visitors in order to improve the customer experience and create a sales funnel to help you convert more visitors.

  3. Analysis + Prediction:

    4. ActiveCampaign makes use of Machine-learning to offer performance metrics and reports but does not quite stop there. It also helps the system to make accurate predictions based on the data gathered. It is reported that its machine learning technology makes more than 1 million predictions every day to help businesses work smarter.

Some of the cons associated with ActiveCampaign are:

  1. No Free Plan:

    2. ActiveCampaign does not offer any free plans, however they do offer a free trial.

  2. Features can be hard to get used to:

    3. With an impressive list of automation and marketing features, ActiveCampaign can be a little difficult to navigate for people who are not very comfortable with technology or are complete beginners in the world of email marketing.

4. Sendinblue

Sendinblue, with its 10 years of industry experience and 175,000 users in over 150 countries is a highly in-demand all-in-one email marketing tool. Sendinblue’s email marketing platform was created to combat one of the biggest challenges faced by businesses, which was cost-effective automation of email marketing.

Pricing:
Free Plan Paid Plan
$0/month (Upto 300 mails per day to unlimited number of subscribers) Starting from $25/month

Some of the stand-out features of Sendinblue are:

  1. Advanced Sign-up form registration:

    2. The registration forms are very advanced, with various personalization features for your email clients. You can let subscribers select which lists they want to be opted into and which lists they are not interested in being added to. You can also enable CAPTCHA verification in order to protect your campaign against bots.

  2. Impressive email automation:

    3. One of Sedinblue's biggest advanced features is the fully automated workflow. Automation workflow can help you to greatly enhance the user experience. You can decide on specific campaign actions using the visitor's email ID and website engagement behaviour and accordingly send transactional messages, promotional emails and much more.

  3. Low budget tool with sophisticated features:

    4. One of the biggest selling points of Sendinblue is that it is a lot cheaper than other comparable tools if you do not plan on sending bulk emails to a fixed group of people. It provides a value-for-money service with impressive workflow automation, dynamic personalization including coming up with ideas of customised subject lines, and an advanced segmentation feature that focuses on sending messages to a more targeted audience from your mailing list.

Some of the cons associated with Sendinblue:

  1. Slow speed of System:

    2. The web application can be a little slow when loading particular pages

  2. The web application can be a little slow when loading particular pages

    3. There is a daily limit to sending messages in their free plan. Sending only 300 daily emails can be a huge drawback to many people.

5. ConvertKit

ConvertKit provides excellent email marketing services to online course creators and promises its potential customers that by signing up with ConvertKit they can focus less on the content marketing aspect and more on the content creation. If you are hoping to sell your online courses, e-books, independent video lessons or other digital products ConvertKit provides an excellent platform for this. You can connect your own custom domain, and add your products with ease. This feature is available on the basic plan which is free. You’ll only be charged a fixed transaction fee once you start selling products.

Pricing:
Free Plan Paid Plan
$0/month (Upto 1000 subscribers) Starting from $29/month

Take a look at the other features this marketing tool offers:

  1. Hassle-free creation of Sign-up forms:

    2. You can create pop-up, embedded, and slide-in forms. You can also create custom landing pages to share your creative projects with your customers. Forms are easy to edit via ConvertKit's visual editor, and there are various attractive designs to choose from.

  2. Basic Analytics:

    3. It is equipped with the basic metrics which include open rates, number of clicks and unsubscribes. There is no e-commerce conversion tracking or social media analysis report. There is no integration of Google Analytics here.

  3. Helps in running super-targeted email campaigns:

    4. You have the provision to create email sequences, and also have access to workflow-based visual automation. After the user triggers an event, for example, they click a link or subscribe to a registration form, you can personalize your communications by adding actions by adding a tag, subscribe them to sequences, target them with valuable content that can convert them from a casual visitor into a loyal customer.

Some of the cons associated with ConvertKit are as follows:

  1. Expensive Service:

    2. When compared to other email marketing tools offering the same features, ConvertKit proves to be a pricey option.

  2. Lack of Designs:

    3. It offers only 3 design templates and all of them are text-based only. There should be more visually appealing template options on offer.
